About

Entrepreneur, this word is part of who I am – however, I didn’t realize this until graduating college. What I foresaw was a highly corporate job; one of repetitive monotonous work. An itch to do more has always been there – especially during hours spent sitting at a desk, staring at a monitor at my previous jobs. It was during those moments that I knew I couldn’t stay in a typical corporate setting.

I embark on my journey of startups for the chance to learn, be challenged, and solve something bigger than myself. It’s clear that failure is high in startups, but I welcome it for a chance to learn from my mistakes. My greatest characteristic, ambition; I am relentless in what I pursue. Success is the goal, and the journey is the fun.
